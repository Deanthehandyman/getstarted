Frequently Asked Questions

Direct Answers About Our Multi-State Technical Services

Do you really travel a 200-mile radius?

Yes. Based in Pittsburg, TX (75686), we travel across East Texas and into Oklahoma, Arkansas, and Louisiana. We specialize in reaching rural locations and off-grid properties that standard local services won't cover.

How do your travel fees work?

The first 100 miles of travel are FREE. For jobs beyond 100 miles, a small trip fee is added to cover fuel and equipment transport. We utilize a mobile workshop to ensure all tools are on-site for remote jobs.

Do you provide the Starlink hardware?

No. You must order your kit directly from Starlink.com. However, if you use our referral link found on our Starlink Hub, you will receive your 2nd Month of Service for FREE.

Do you work on RVs?

Absolutely. Dean is an expert in RV connectivity and maintenance. We specialize in Starlink Mini 12V DC conversions and magnetic vehicle mounts for travelers who need reliable internet without an inverter.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. We are a fully registered LLC and carry professional liability insurance. We operate at a licensed-professional level in our technical trades, ensuring all work meets safety and structural standards.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major secure digital payments, including Square, Venmo, PayPal, and Cash App. Secure links for payments and deposits can be found on our homepage and in our invoices.
