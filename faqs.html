Common Questions

Straight answers about our services, pricing, and coverage.

ðŸ“¡ Starlink & Internet
Do you sell the Starlink kit?
No. You must purchase the Starlink kit directly from Starlink.com or authorized retailers like Best Buy. I provide the professional installation, custom mounting hardware, and cable routing services.
Can you install if I have tall trees?
Yes. This is my specialty in East Texas. I can install tower mounts, tree-toppers, or chimney mounts to clear the canopy and get a signal where others fail. I perform an obstruction test before drilling any holes.
Will you drill holes in my roof?
I avoid roof penetrations whenever possible by using fascia mounts or cable entry via soffit vents. If a roof penetration is required, I use industrial-grade pitch pads and sealants to guarantee a leak-proof result.

Do you do full panel upgrades?
No. I focus on "downstream" repairs: outlets, switches, fixtures, fans, and sub-panels for sheds. For a main 200-amp service panel replacement, I recommend hiring a Master Electrician for permitting purposes.
Can you mount a TV above a fireplace?
Yes. I can drill into brick or stone and use heavy-duty anchors. I also provide in-wall wire concealment so you don't see messy cords hanging down.
