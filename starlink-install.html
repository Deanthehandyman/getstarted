Why 12V DC Conversion?

Standard Starlink routers run on AC power. Converting to a native 12V DC PoE injector allows your RV to power the dish directly, eliminating the "phantom draw" of an inverter and keeping your internet live even when boondocking.

Obstruction Surveys
