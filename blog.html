Storm Prep 101: Securing Your Starlink & Property

When storm season hits the Ark-La-Tex, wind speeds can easily top 60mph. If your satellite dish, antennas, or outdoor fixtures aren't secured with industrial fasteners, they become projectiles.

The "Wiggle Test"

Go outside and check your mounts. If you can wiggle your Starlink mast by hand, the wind will rip it off. I use heavy-duty lag bolts and structural reinforcement for every install to ensure your internet stays up even when the weather goes down.
