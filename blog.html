February 6, 2026 | Starlink Installation

Why Professional Mounting is Critical in the Piney Woods

In Pittsburg, TX and surrounding areas, trees are the primary enemy of high-speed satellite internet. While the Starlink Gen 3 "kickstand" works for clear fields, East Texas homes usually require a higher vantage point.

Overcoming Obstructions

We specialize in custom roof-peak mounts and pole installations that clear the tree line. Even a small obstruction can cause major dropouts. We use industrial-grade pivot mounts to find the exact "sweet spot" for your dish.

Industrial Cabling

We don't just "run a wire." We use commercial-grade cable routing and interior wall plates to protect your connection from the elements and pests.

February 4, 2026 | Service Standards

From Embalming to Industrial Trades: Why Precision Matters

My background is unique—I’ve worked as a funeral director and embalmer, as well as in heavy construction involving hydraulics and fabrication. What do these have in common? Precision.

No Room for Error

Whether I am ensuring a sanitary and precise environment in a funeral home or wiring a smart security system, the stakes are high. I bring that same level of meticulous care to your home repairs. When I install an electrical fixture or route data cables, I’m not just looking for "functional"—I’m looking for "flawless."

January 29, 2026 | RV Technology

Optimizing Your RV Pads for 2026 Tech Standards

I’ve developed my own property in Pittsburg to include RV pads and a handyman shop. Having lived full-time in a 40-foot Forest River Wildwood, I know exactly what an RV owner needs for long-term comfort.

Power & Connectivity

We help property owners install proper electrical pedestals and high-speed Wi-Fi infrastructure. For travelers, we specialize in Starlink Mini 12V DC power conversions, allowing you to stay connected off-grid without needing a massive inverter.
